Dinky vs PDF Squeezer

PDF Squeezer batch-compresses PDFs natively on the Mac. Dinky covers similar shrink for free, open source, and folds in images and video in one dock icon.

Choose PDF Squeezer if PDF-only fits your stack

When you want a polished single-purpose PDF product from Witt Software and do not need image or video in the same tool.

Choose Dinky for PDFs plus media in one MIT app

When the same inbox mixes PDFs, PNG/JPEG/WebP, and MP4—and you prefer inspectable code and zero purchase.
