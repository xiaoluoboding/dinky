Dinky vs Permute

Permute converts video, audio, and images with polish. Dinky chases smaller bytes and real PDF workflows—with audio cross-converts—in a free MIT app.

Choose Permute for broad format conversion

When you routinely convert between many container and codec combinations and want a dedicated converter app for that breadth.

Choose Dinky for shrink-first images, video, audio, and PDFs

When the priority is smaller files—and audio conversions alongside visuals—plus open source, watch folders, and PDF flatten/preserve—not every possible conversion matrix.
