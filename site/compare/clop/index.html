Dinky vs Clop

Clop lives in the menu bar—clipboard, quick tweaks; Pro adds automation. Dinky’s a free drop zone with watch folders, deeper PDF paths, and a smaller footprint.

Choose Clop if the menu bar is home base

When you want clipboard-first capture, quick actions from the bar, and are fine with Clop’s tiering for automation—keep using what matches your muscle memory.

Choose Dinky for OSS, drop zone, and PDF depth

When you want inspectable source, no purchase, batch + watch folders without a Pro gate, and flatten/preserve PDF workflows beside FPS-capped video, audio cross‑converts, and stills.
