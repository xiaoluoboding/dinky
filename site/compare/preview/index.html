Dinky vs Preview

Preview exports a leaner PDF now and then. Dinky batches, watches folders, and adds scan OCR when Preview runs out of road—plus images and video.

Choose Preview for quick one-off exports

When you already have the PDF open, need a smaller copy once, and the built-in export dialog is enough.

Choose Dinky for batch PDF and mixed media

When you drop folders of PDFs, want watch folders, OCR-aware scans, or the same session to include MP4 and still images.
